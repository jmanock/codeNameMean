Fantasy Golf Challenge

How to play

  1. Each team selects four golfers for each tournament
  2. Your team score is all golfers points added together
  3. Golfers get points based on a modified stableford system

    4. 4 Points for EAGLE or Better 2 Points for BIRDIE
    0 Points for PAR
    -1 Points for BOGIE
    -2 Points for DOUBLE or Worse
